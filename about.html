About the App

Our Goal?

The current energy crisis and climate change necessitate lowering our energy consumption and CO2 emissions, and transportation plays a significant role in this process. This application helps users comprehend the impact of their transportation choices and motivates them to make more sustainable choices by providing information on their energy consumption and CO2 emissions.

 

How it works?

Tracking

  1. Select the user in the top left corner.
  2. Choose your transportation mode with and start the trip in the bottom left corner.
  3. The app is now tracking. To switch your transportation mode, press "Switch Mode" and choose your mode.
  4. To end your trip, press the end trip button. The data will be submitted to the database.
  5. To view your daily energy consumption and CO2 emissions, press

Feedback

  1. Choose the date and the user. Press "Show query" to display your energy consumption and emissions
  2. The map shows your trajectory of that day
  3. The table on the bottom of the page shows the total MegaJoule consumption, the total CO2 emissions and the total kilometrs travelled.

 

Calculations?

To calculate energy consumption and CO2 emissions, we utilize data from mobitool.ch. It is important to note that we use average values and the results may not perfectly reflect your actual consumption and emissions.
